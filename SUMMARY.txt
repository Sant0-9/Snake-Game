================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Problem: Modern version exits immediately
Solution: Use simple version instead (make run-linux)

Problem: No colors showing
Solution: Your terminal might not support ANSI colors (game still works)

Problem: Controls not working
Solution: Press ENTER at welcome screen, or use simple version

Problem: make command not found
Solution: Build manually with g++ command above
